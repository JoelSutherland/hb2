HB2 Hurts North Carolina

In an emergency session held on March 23, North Carolina Legislators passed a bill preventing cities, towns and counties in North Carolina from passing anti-discrimination laws to protect LGBT citizens. The bill has already been condemned by businesses and corporations inside and outside of North Carolina and many others continue to express their opposition.
